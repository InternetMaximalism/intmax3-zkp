1 · Open a channel (as a delegate)

You join as a send-only delegate: this generates your keys (Goldilocks + BabyBear hash-sig + Regev) and funds your slot, while the local CLI runs the three co-signing members (slots 0–2). The members sign the channel state; you only sign your own sends. All proving/lattice work happens in your browser.

2 · Your balance

—

Re-imports the latest channel state — click after someone sends you funds to see them arrive. (After receiving, a balance refresh is needed before you can send again.)

3 · Send (as the delegate)

As the delegate you authorize the send with your OWN BabyBear hash-sig (A11) over the Regev E-1 zero-knowledge proof (~3ms) — built in your browser. The three members then co-sign the resulting state (each a Goldilocks SingleSig ZK proof); they will only co-sign a debit you signed.

The wait is the in-browser ZK proving — the sender's BabyBear Poseidon2 hash-sig proof (Plonky3) plus circuit setup; the Regev STARK itself is ~3ms. The button shows elapsed time.
