3 · Send (as the delegate)

As the delegate you authorize the send with your OWN BabyBear hash-sig (A11) over the Regev E-1 zero-knowledge proof (~3ms) — built in your browser. The three members then co-sign the resulting state (each a Goldilocks SingleSig ZK proof); they will only co-sign a debit you signed.

The wait is the in-browser ZK proving — the sender's BabyBear Poseidon2 hash-sig proof (Plonky3) plus circuit setup; the Regev STARK itself is ~3ms. The button shows elapsed time.
